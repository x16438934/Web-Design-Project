Interim Title Fight

Mcgregor was supposed to have fought Jose Aldo for the FeatherWeight belt in July 11th 2015. Unfortunately Aldo pulled out due to a sore rib 11 days before the fight. Chad Mendes was chosen to fight Mcgregor for the Interim belt.

Mcgregor Vs Mendes Highlights

FeatherWeight Title Fight

McGregor finally got his hands on Jose Aldo early December 2015 and the results were phenomenal. Mcgregor knocked out Aldo who hasn't been beaten in 10 year with just one punch, given Mcgregor the fastest title fight KO in just 13 seconds.

Mcgregor vs Aldo

LightWeight Title Fight

Mcgregor faced Eddie Alvarez at UFC 205 for the Lightweight title belt. It was a surprise to most people when Conor destroyed Alvarez with ease. Mcgregor knocked out Alvarez in the 2nd round with a devastating 4 combo punch.

Mcgregor vs Alvarez Highlights

LightWeight Fight

Conor Mcgregor shook the world when he destroyed Eddie Alvarez at UFC 205 to make him the undisputed two time lightweight and featherweight champion of the world. He is the first man to hold two belts at the exact same time. Unfortunately the UFC have stripped him of his featherweight belt but Conor is yet to comment on it.

FeatherWeight Fight

A very anticipated fight between Mcgregor and Aldo was due to happen after Aldo had already pulled out of the contest 2 weeks before the fight due to a rib injury. When the fight was rescheduled Mcgregor was the favourite going into the fight by the fans but not the fighters. Most fighters assumed Aldo would toy with Mgregor, but that wasnt the case. Mcgregor knocked Aldo out in one punch in 13 seconds given him the fastest finish in a championship fight.

Wealth and Fortune

Conor Mcgregor is without a doubt the richest fighter on the UFC roster taking the spotlight off Ronda Rousey and Brock Lesnar. He posts his fortunes on Facebook and Twitter and we can truely see how much the man makes by his range in expensive suits, watches and cars. Conor Mcgreor has paid off his parents mortgages and has bought them all new cars and merchantise since becoming a millionaire.
